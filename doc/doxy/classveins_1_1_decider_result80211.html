double veins::DeciderResult80211::recvPower_dBm

Stores the received power in dBm Please note that this is NOT the RSSI.

The RSSI is an indicator of the quality of the signal which is not standardized, and different vendors can define different indicators. This value indicates the power that the frame had when received by the NIC card, WITHOUT noise floor and WITHOUT interference
